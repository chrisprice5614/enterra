Npc testing!

As of late, lots of new NPCs have been modelled, rigged and animated into the game. The AI is unique for every single enemy, letting each of them have their challenge to take on. Enemies are mainly locked to their location (ie. Grassland, Lavalands, etc..) but some enemies will appear in multiple areas. Just trying to make sure this game feels as fun and as fresh as possible :)
